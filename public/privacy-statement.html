Privacy statement

Lilypond source entered in Hacklily is rendered using a copy of LilyPond running on a server. The server may store a copy of your song for debugging or analytics for up to 7 days. This is currently the only information we collect.

By connecting Hacklily to GitHub, you agree to GitHub's privacy statement. Connecting Hacklily to GitHub allows you to save, store, and publish songs on GitHub and GitHub Pages.

EVERYTHING YOU SAVE ON HACKLILY WILL BE PUBLICALLY VIEWABLE UNDER YOUR GITHUB ACCOUNT. It will be publically stored on a GitHub repo associated with your GitHub account under the hacklily organization. All history will be stored. You can delete your history by removing the Hacklily repo associated with your account, or by contacting joshua@nettek.ca.

Hacklily's privacy statement may change without notice. These changes will appear on this website.

You may contact the privacy officer by email at joshua@nettek.ca.
